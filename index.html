Intro

Hello! My name is Robert and I am a Full Stack Javascript Developer. I have both personal and educational experience that has driven me to where I am at today. I owe LambdaSchool a large shout out as I would not be here today with out their guidance and support.

Since 2016, I have been using various websites such as codecademy, Pluralsight, freecodecamp.org, and a few others to teach myself HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I have been a student at LambdaSchool since late February of 2019. I encourage you to visit my awesome portfolio or my Github page to see what I have been working on most recently!


I would love to start a conversation with you from anything from the weather in your local area to a project you would like my assistance with, please contact me anytime!
